    • The Great Theory of Progress in Osu!

    i.e How to Apply Bloom's Taxonomy to Boost Your Results.

    Nowadays, many people devote their time to computer games. Some have a casual approach to gaming, while others have a need to compete with others. Osu!, however, is a type of game that allows players to do both approaches. For some, however, Osu! provides a place to be fascinated by various processes, in my case the processes of improving one’s own skills.

    By using Bloom’s taxonomy as an analytical tool, we will be able to delve into individual aspects of the game to be able to identify specific skills that need improvement and understand their aspects. Bloom's taxonomy is a classification of levels of thinking and learning that can be used to develop goals and tasks. It consists of six levels, the so-called Bloom pyramid: remember, evaluate, apply, analyze, evaluate and create. Unlike unsystematic attempts at learning, every level of Bloom's taxonomy requires.

    critical thinking. In order to achieve the goals of the project, we will break down into the first factors all necessary for the progress of the skills in the game. Then we assign them to the appropriate levels of Bloom's taxonomy. In addition, everything will be reviewed through community feedback to understand where you can expand the design or correct errors in the thesis.
